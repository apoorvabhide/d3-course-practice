Why Data Science?

The aspect of Data Science that has attracted me the most is the interdisciplinarity.
For example, we are currently a team of data analysts working across fields like Retail, Shopping Malls, and Cinemas.
Even in my own free time, I've completed projects on Customer sentiment, on Cricket, which is a sport I love dearly, as well as with forecasting energy consumption.
That's what I've loved about data science - it's a set of tools that can be customised to understand and help any domain.
